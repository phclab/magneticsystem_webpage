Sensors

4 sensors were used in this study, including the Hall sensor, decibel sensor, acceleration sensor, and temperature sensor.

Hall Sensor

SS495A(Honeywell)

Supply voltage: 4.5V to 10.5V

Supply ampere: 7mA

Detect Range: -67mT to 67mT

Sensitivity: 3.125±0.125 mV/G

Vibration Sensor

ADXL345(Analog Devices)

Supply voltage: 3V to 5V

Supply ampere: 0.03mA

Detect Range: ±16g

Sensitivity: 4 mg/LSB

Temperature Sensor

TMP36GT9Z(Analog Devices)

Supply voltage: 2.7V to 5.5V

Supply ampere: 0.02mA

Detect Range: -40°C to 125°C

Sensitivity: ±2°C

Sound Sensor

SEN0232(TaiwanIOT)

Supply voltage: 3.3V to 5.0V

Supply ampere: 14mA

Detect Range: 30dBA to 130dBA

Sensitivity: ±1.5dB
